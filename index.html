(Just Like) Starting Over

December 31, 2019

If you're able to read this then I managed to reboot my personal site without too much drama. Things are a little bare at the moment - not much in the way of musings and only a few, select portfolio projects are available for perusal over in that section of the site - but with a new decade comes new opportunities for change and growth. Of course the first task of real importance is to finish Gatsby-fying things around here. And yes, 'Gatsby-fying' is an actual, totally cromulent word.
